## 🚀 Who We Are

**Vizipp** is a global technology company on a mission to transform businesses through innovative digital solutions. Headquartered in Texas, Vizipp combines **cutting-edge software development, AI-powered systems, and tailored IT services** to help organizations optimize operations, streamline workflows, and unlock digital potential.

We serve clients across industries — from education and corporate sectors to government organizations — by delivering robust web and mobile applications, data analytics, automation platforms, and strategic digital tools.
